# Backlinks Strategy — 90-Day Playbook for Dakiraty / Coach Ahmed

Backlinks (other websites linking to yours) are roughly **30-40% of Google's ranking signal**. You can publish 200 perfect articles, but if zero credible Arabic sites link to you, Google treats you as unknown.

This document is the concrete playbook: target sites, outreach templates, weekly rhythm, and what NOT to do.

---

## The 90-day target

| Month | Backlinks goal | Effort |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Month 1 | 5-8 quality links | 3-5 hours/week outreach |
| Month 2 | 15-20 total | Steady weekly outreach + 1 guest post |
| Month 3 | 30-40 total | Outreach + 2-3 guest posts |
| End of Year 1 | 80-150 quality links | Foundation for serious ranking |

Quality > quantity, always. One link from `aljazeera.net` beats 100 links from spam directories.

These don't pass full SEO value but drive direct traffic + brand visibility:

- **Reddit r/arabs, r/morocco, r/islam:** participate genuinely, link sparingly.

**Rule:** never spam. One in every 5 contributions can link to your site. The other 4 just add value.

## Topics that work best as guest posts

Pitch these to relevant sites:

1. **"كيف تحفظ الأسماء والوجوه في الاجتماعات"** → business/professional sites
2. **"7 أطعمة تقوي ذاكرة طفلك"** → parenting sites
3. **"كيف يحفظ أبطال العالم 500 رقم في ساعة"** → general curiosity sites
4. **"الذاكرة قبل الامتحان: خطة عملية"** → student/education sites
5. **"حفظ القرآن للموظفين المشغولين"** → Islamic education sites
6. **"التركيز في عصر TikTok"** → digital wellness / tech sites
7. **"النوم والذاكرة: ما يقوله العلم"** → health/wellness sites

For each: **never republish your existing article**. Write a fresh version, with different examples and a unique angle for that audience. Link back to a related article on your site (not just the homepage).

## Weekly rhythm (sustainable)

### Monday — Research (1-2 hours)
- Identify 5 new outreach targets
- Read their content to find a personalized angle
- Add to your tracking spreadsheet

### Tuesday-Wednesday — Outreach (2-3 hours)
- Send 5 personalized emails (never copy-paste templates as-is)
- Use template as base, add specific reference to their content
- Track sent date, response status

### Thursday — Content for outreach (2 hours)
- Write or refine a guest post pitch
- Draft a piece tailored for a specific site

### Friday — Engagement (1 hour)
- Quora answers, Reddit replies, comments on industry blogs
- Genuine value, link only when 100% relevant

### Saturday — Review & adjust
- Check what got responses, what was ignored
- Refine templates based on actual results

## What NOT to do (these get you penalized)

❌ **Buy backlinks from cheap services** ("100 links for $20"). Google detects these in days.

❌ **Mass-comment spam** on blogs ("Great post! Visit my site").

❌ **Private Blog Networks (PBN)** — networks of fake sites linking to yours. Major penalty risk.

❌ **Exact-match anchor text overuse** — if 80% of your backlinks say "تقوية الذاكرة", that's a manipulation signal.

❌ **Reciprocal link schemes** — "I'll link to you if you link to me" en masse looks unnatural.

❌ **Forum signature spam** — old technique, now penalized.

❌ **Press release blasts** — paying $50 to publish a PR on 100 sites. Worthless and risky.

## Tools to use

### Free
- **Google Search Console** — see who's already linking to you
- **Ubersuggest** (free tier) — competitor backlink analysis
- **Hunter.io** (free tier) — find email addresses of site editors
- **MailTracker** (Gmail extension) — see who opens your outreach emails

### Paid (only if scaling)
- **Ahrefs** ($99/month) — best backlink analysis
- **SEMrush** ($129/month) — alternative
- **Pitchbox** ($595/month) — outreach automation

**For Month 1-3, free tools are enough.** Don't pay for tools until you've proven the strategy works.

## Realistic expectations

- **70-80% of your outreach emails will be ignored**. Normal. Don't take it personally.
- **10-15% will reply but decline.** Polite no's. Move on.
- **5-10% will say yes.** This is your win rate.
- **Quality > quantity** of attempts. 5 deeply personalized emails > 50 templated ones.

---

## First 3 actions to take this week

1. **Set up Google Search Console** for the site (5 min, free).
2. **Make a list of 20 target sites** (1 hour).
3. **Send 3 highly personalized outreach emails** (2 hours). Use template 1 or 2. Reference specific articles on their site.

That's it. Start small. Repeat weekly. Backlinks compound.
